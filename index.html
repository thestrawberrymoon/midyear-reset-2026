Part One · Reflect

The first half
of the year

What moments from the first half will you remember years from now?

Think of moments, not milestones. A walk, a conversation, a morning.

How are you feeling, honestly?

Not how you're supposed to feel. How you actually feel right now.

What surprised you?

About the world, about the year, about yourself.

What are you proud of — even if no one else noticed?

Small things count. The habit you kept. The thing you finally started.

What drained you?

The thing that kept taking more than it gave.

What gave you energy?

The thing that made you feel most like yourself. Notice it.
